Privacy Policy

SCRUB REPORT PRIVACY POLICY Last Updated: March 10, 2026 ScrubReport ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT Account Information: - Email address (for sign-up and sign-in) - Username (chosen by you, 3-30 characters) - Avatar style preference User-Submitted Content: - Hospital reviews (ratings, review text, department/unit) - Pay reports (base hourly pay, shift differential, weekly gross, notes) - Your healthcare role, employment type, and experience level - Content reports when flagging inappropriate reviews - Hospital edit suggestions We Do NOT Collect: - Your real name (unless provided via Apple Sign In) - Phone number or physical address - Device identifiers or advertising IDs (IDFA) - Location data from your device - Contacts, photos, or calendar data 2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA - To operate the app and display reviews and pay data - To authenticate your account - To enforce community guidelines and moderate content - To prevent spam and abuse via rate limiting - To process your Pro subscription 3. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY Your data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted infrastructure). Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. Row Level Security ensures users can only access their own private data. Reviews and pay data are displayed anonymously — your user ID is never shown publicly. 4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES Google AdMob: We display banner ads to free users via Google AdMob. We do not request tracking permission (ATT), so AdMob operates in limited mode. AdMob may automatically collect your IP address, device model, and OS version for ad serving. Pro subscribers do not see ads. Apple StoreKit: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We do not handle or store payment information. Apple Sign In: If you sign in with Apple, we receive only your email address and an authentication token. DiceBear: Avatar images are generated using the DiceBear API with a random seed — no personal data is sent. 5. DATA SHARING We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app. 6. ANONYMITY All reviews and pay reports are displayed anonymously. The public views of reviews strip your user ID so other users cannot identify who wrote a review. 7. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION You can delete your account at any time from within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, reviews, and pay reports from our database. 8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY ScrubReport is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children. 9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on our website. 10.
