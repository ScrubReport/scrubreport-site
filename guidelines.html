Community Guidelines

SCRUB REPORT COMMUNITY GUIDELINES Last Updated: March 10, 2026 ScrubReport exists to help healthcare workers make informed career decisions through honest, anonymous reviews and transparent pay data. These guidelines keep our community safe and useful. DO: - Share honest, firsthand experiences from hospitals where you have worked - Report accurate pay data to the best of your knowledge - Keep reviews focused on workplace conditions, staffing, management, culture, pay, and safety - Use the report feature to flag content that violates these guidelines - Be respectful of fellow healthcare workers DO NOT: - Include names or identifying details of coworkers, managers, doctors, or patients - Post phone numbers, email addresses, social media handles, or other contact information - Use profanity, slurs, hate speech, or discriminatory language - Submit fake or fabricated reviews or pay data - Review a hospital where you have not worked - Use the app to harass, threaten, or retaliate against individuals - Create multiple accounts to manipulate ratings - Post patient information of any kind (this also violates HIPAA) CONTENT MODERATION: Our app automatically screens submissions for: - Personally identifying information (names, emails, phone numbers) - Profane and offensive language - Suspicious patterns (rate limiting, duplicate submissions) The community can report reviews for: - Identifying information - Inappropriate content - Spam or fake reviews - Harassment or threats - Clearly inaccurate information Content that receives multiple community reports is automatically flagged and may be removed. ENFORCEMENT: Violations may result in: - Content removal - Account suspension - Permanent account termination We review flagged content and take action to maintain a safe, trustworthy community. REMEMBER: Your reviews help real people make real career decisions. Keep them honest, anonymous, and constructive. General criticism of departments, management, staffing, and workplace conditions is encouraged — just leave specific individuals out of it. Questions or concerns: scrubreporthq@gmail.com
