Community Guidelines

SCRUB REPORT COMMUNITY GUIDELINES Community Guidelines Last updated: March 18, 2026 ScrubReport exists so healthcare workers can share honest workplace experiences and help each other find better jobs. These guidelines keep the platform useful and trustworthy. 1. Be Honest Only review workplaces where you have personally worked or are currently working. Share your genuine experience. Don't exaggerate, fabricate, or post reviews on behalf of someone else. If your experience changed over time, your review should reflect that nuance. 2. Protect Privacy Never name individual people — not coworkers, managers, doctors, patients, or anyone else. Refer to roles instead (e.g., "my charge nurse" or "the unit manager"). Never share patient information. Any content that could violate HIPAA will be removed immediately. Don't share your own identity in reviews. ScrubReport is designed to be anonymous — keep it that way. 3. Stay Constructive It's fine to be critical. It's not fine to be cruel. Focus on workplace conditions, management practices, pay, staffing, and culture — not personal attacks on individuals. Reviews that are purely venting without any useful information may be removed. 4. What's Not Allowed The following content will be removed and may result in account suspension: False or fabricated reviews — reviewing a hospital where you never worked Personal attacks — naming or identifying specific individuals HIPAA violations — any patient information, no matter how vague Discrimination or hate speech — targeting people based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any protected characteristic Threats or harassment — threatening violence or encouraging harm Spam or solicitation — advertising, recruiting, or self-promotion Duplicate or copy-paste reviews — posting the same review across multiple hospitals Retaliation reviews — posting fake negative reviews to harm a specific facility as payback for a personal dispute 5. Pay Data Only submit your own compensation data. Don't guess or post someone else's pay. Be accurate — the community depends on reliable data to understand fair pay. Include the right employment type (staff, PRN, travel) and experience level so the data is useful for comparison. 6. Reporting and Moderation Use the in-app flag/report button to report content that violates these guidelines. Our moderation team reviews flagged content and takes action as needed. We reserve the right to remove any content and suspend any account at our discretion. We won't always explain moderation decisions in detail, but we aim to be fair and consistent. 7. Consequences Violations may result in: Content removal Warning Temporary suspension Permanent account ban Severe violations (HIPAA breaches, threats, doxxing) will result in immediate permanent ban. The bottom line: Write the review you wish existed when you were deciding whether to take a job at that hospital. Be honest, be helpful, and respect everyone's privacy.
